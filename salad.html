🍝 Pasta

⏱ 20 minutes  |  🍽 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Boil a pot of salted water
  2. Cook pasta for 10 minutes until al dente
  3. Fry garlic in olive oil for 2 minutes
  4. Drain pasta, mix with garlic oil
  5. Top with parmesan and serve
