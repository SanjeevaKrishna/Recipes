🥗 Salad

⏱ 10 minutes  |  🍽 2 servings

Ingredients

  • 2 cups lettuce
  • 1 tomato, chopped
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice, salt

Steps

  1. Wash all vegetables thoroughly
  2. Chop tomato and slice cucumber
  3. Tear lettuce into a large bowl
  4. Add tomato and cucumber on top
  5. Drizzle olive oil and lemon juice, serve
